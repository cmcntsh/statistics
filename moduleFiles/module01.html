Objectives

  1. Get to know everyone in the class
  2. Understand the relationship between informatics and statistics
  3. Understand how statistics are used in general
  4. Get RStudio set up on your computer
  5. Learn how to open a dataset in RStudio
  6. Understand levels of measurement and types of variables
